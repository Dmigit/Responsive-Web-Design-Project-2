Our Mission

Our mission is to be the trusted technology and capacity provider of the global logic IC industry for years to come.

Our Products

Production on our 7 nanometer chips began in 2017, and was first released as Apple's A12 bionic chip in their iphone XS series

Production on our 5 nanometer chips began in 2020, and was first used as Apple's A14 bionic chip in their iphone 12 series, and offers 15% higher speed and 30% less power conaumprion than it's predecessor

Production on our 3 nanometer chips began in 2022, and will first be released as Apple's A16 chip in their iphone 14 series. Our 3 nanometer chips are 15% faster and 30% more efficient than our N5 chips
